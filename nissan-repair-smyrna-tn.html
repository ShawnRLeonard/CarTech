Nissan Services We Cover

🔍

Diagnostics & Check Engine

Check engine light, TPMS warning, CVT error codes, ABS light, electrical faults. We read Nissan-specific codes and tell you exactly what's happening — no guesswork, no parts-throwing.

🕔

Routine Maintenance

Oil changes (conventional, synthetic, or full synthetic), brake pads and rotors, tire rotation and balance, cabin and engine air filters, coolant flush. We follow your Nissan's factory service schedule.

⚙️

Engine & Drivetrain

Timing chain, head gaskets, spark plugs, serpentine belts, water pumps, and engine mounts. Nissan's VQ and MR engines are well-known to us — including the common Altima timing chain issues.

🚘

CVT & Transmission

Nissan's CVT transmissions need fluid changes on a strict schedule. We service CVTs, automatic, and manual transmissions — plus minor repairs and full replacements when needed.

🌟

Paint & Collision Repair

Fender benders, parking lot dings, side swipes, or full collision repair — our dedicated body shop uses computerized color matching to blend your Nissan's finish perfectly. Free estimates.

🔊

Noise & Drivability

Rattles, shuddering, vibration at speed, rough idle, or a CVT that hesitates — we track down the source of the problem precisely. No guessing, no unnecessary repairs.
