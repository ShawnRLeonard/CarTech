Auto Body & Paint Shop in Smyrna, TN

Whether your vehicle was in a collision, picked up a parking lot ding, or needs a fresh coat of paint, CarTech Automotive has the tools and experience to bring it back to its best. In 2024 we expanded our shop with a dedicated, state-of-the-art paint booth — a climate-controlled environment that produces a factory-quality finish every time.

We handle everything from minor dents and scratches to major structural collision repairs. Our technicians use computerized color matching to identify your vehicle's exact factory color code and mix paint that blends seamlessly with the surrounding panels. We don't rush, we don't cut corners, and we won't return your vehicle until it looks right.

CarTech is a family-owned shop, not a franchise. Emmanuel and Ruben Salazar built this business on doing quality work for their community — and that standard applies to every single job that rolls through our paint booth door, no matter the size.
