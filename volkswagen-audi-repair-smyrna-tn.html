VW & Audi Services We Cover

🔍

Diagnostics & Check Engine

DSG fault codes, timing chain stretch, high-pressure fuel pump (HPFP) codes on TSI/TFSI engines, carbon buildup on direct-injection engines, and all VW/Audi-specific faults diagnosed accurately.

🕔

Routine Maintenance

Oil changes to VW 502.00/504.00 spec or Audi longlife spec, brake service, tire rotation, spark plugs, cabin pollen filter, and air filters. We follow VW/Audi's Flexible Service Schedule intervals.

⚙️

TSI/TFSI Engine Service

Timing chain and tensioner service (EA888 timing chain failure is a known VW/Audi issue), carbon cleaning on direct-injection engines, water pump, thermostat, and all EA211/EA888 engine repairs.

🚘

DSG & S tronic Service

DSG 7-speed (DQ200) and 6-speed (DQ250) fluid and filter service using OEM-spec G052182 fluid — this fluid specification matters and the wrong product will damage the mechatronics unit. Audi S tronic service included.

🌟

Paint & Collision Repair

Fender dings, bumper scuffs, or full collision repair — our dedicated body shop uses computerized color matching for all VW and Audi colors, including Deep Black Pearl and complex metallics. Free estimates.

🔊

Noise & Drivability

DSG shudder on low-speed engagement, timing chain rattle, rough cold start on direct injection — VW/Audi-specific issues that need accurate diagnosis. We find the source and fix it right.
