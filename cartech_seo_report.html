3. Recurring Local SEO Operations Checklist

SEO isn't a one-time project; Google prioritizes active, highly-rated profiles. Perform the following recurring tasks weekly to build local search momentum:

  • Google Business Profile (GBP) Photo Engine (Weekly) Upload 1-2 new photos directly to your GBP every week. Google loves active shops. What to post: Before/after collision paint work, vehicles lifted on lifts, close-ups of engine diagnostics, and team photos. Do not use stock photos.
  • Review Request Text campaign (Post-Service) Review volume and frequency are key indicators for the Google Maps Pack. Make requesting a review part of the vehicle pickup process. Template: "Hi [Name], thank you for trusting CarTech with your [Vehicle] today! If you have 30 seconds, could you leave Scott, Ruben & Emmanuel a review? It helps our family shop out a lot: [Your Review Link]"
  • Keyword-Optimized Review Replies (As Received) When responding to customer reviews, naturally write the location and services performed. This feeds Google's local search index directly.
Review Reply Guide & Examples:

Rule: Don't just say "Thanks for the review!" Say where they are and what service you provided.

Don't Reply Like This:

"Thanks John for the 5-star review! We appreciate your business and hope to see you again soon."

Do Reply Like This:

"Thanks John! Glad we could take care of your Toyota Camry brake service here in Smyrna. Drive safely!"
