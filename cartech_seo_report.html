Overview

The CarTech website runs on GoHighLevel and looks presentable, but is missing critical SEO elements that directly affect placement in Google's Map Pack � the top local results customers click when searching "auto repair Smyrna TN" or "car repair near me." Every day these items are unfixed is a day competitors with better-configured sites rank higher and get that call instead.

SEO Audit � cartechsmyrna.com (Live Site)

Element Status Why It Matters
Title tag Good Includes "Smyrna TN" + service keywords. Solid.
Meta description Good Present and location-targeted. Shows under site name in Google results.
Open Graph tags (social) Good Controls appearance when site is shared on Facebook/social media.
JSON-LD Business Schema MISSING ? Highest priority. Tells Google your exact name, address, phone, hours, and business type. Without it Google guesses � and competitors with it rank above you in the map pack.
Google Analytics 4 MISSING No visitor tracking on the site at all. We have zero data on traffic, where customers come from, or what's working.
Canonical tag Missing Without it Google may treat "www." and non-"www." as competing pages and split the ranking signal.
Meta robots tag Missing Should explicitly tell Google to index and follow the site.
Google Search Console Unknown Unclear if site has been submitted. Without it, we can't see indexing errors or submit a sitemap.
Google Business Profile Unknown Most direct driver of calls and directions. Unclear if current team is actively managing it (photos, reviews, posts).
Favicon Generic Shows GoHighLevel's icon in browser tabs, not the CarTech brand.
Mobile zoom / accessibility Issue Site blocks users from zooming on mobile (user-scalable=no). Google penalizes this in accessibility scoring.
Keyword typos in metadata Minor "Collison repair" (misspelled) and "care maintenance" appear in the site's hidden metadata.

A Note on Zeely (AI-Powered Organic Growth App)

Zeely is a mobile app marketed to small businesses for creating AI-generated social media content, ads, and brand materials quickly. Here is an honest take on whether it fits CarTech:

Where Zeely Helps
  • Fast social media post generation � good for keeping Facebook/Instagram active without spending hours on design
  • Non-designers can produce decent-looking graphics quickly
  • AI ad copy suggestions for boosting posts or running simple campaigns
  • Lower cost than hiring a social media manager ($20�$40/mo range)
Where Zeely Falls Short
  • Does nothing for technical SEO or Google search rankings
  • Generic AI content � still needs Shawn's edits to sound like CarTech, not a template
  • No website editing, no structured data, no Google tools integration
  • Another subscription to manage; not a replacement for the work outlined in this report
Verdict: Zeely is a decent helper for social media content if CarTech wants to post more consistently on Facebook and Instagram without spending much time on it. It is not an SEO tool and won't move the needle on Google search rankings or the map pack. Use it as a supplement � not a strategy. The technical fixes in this report come first.
