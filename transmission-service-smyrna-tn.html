Transmission Service & Repair in Smyrna, TN

Your transmission is responsible for getting power from your engine to your wheels. When it starts slipping, shifting rough, or leaking fluid, those aren't problems you can ignore — they tend to get worse quickly and more expensive the longer they go unaddressed.

At CarTech Automotive, we service automatic, manual, and CVT transmissions on all makes and models. We start with a thorough diagnostic to understand exactly what's happening before recommending any repair. Sometimes it's a fluid service. Sometimes it's a specific component repair. And sometimes a replacement is the honest answer. We'll tell you which — clearly and without pressure.

Don't wait until a slipping gear turns into a complete transmission failure. Bring it in early and let us tell you where things actually stand.

Why Choose CarTech for Transmission Services

  • Fluid changes with correct OEM-spec fluids — transmission fluid is not one-size-fits-all. We use the right fluid for your specific make, model, and transmission type.
  • Automatic, manual, and CVT service — we service all transmission types including CVTs common on Nissan, Subaru, Honda, and Toyota models.
  • Diagnostic-first approach — we identify the actual problem before recommending a repair. We won't sell you a full rebuild when a fluid service or minor repair is what's needed.
  • Repairs and full replacements — from targeted repairs to complete transmission replacements, we provide honest options and let you decide.
  • All makes and models — domestic and import. One shop for everything, no runaround.
  • Transparent pricing — full estimate in writing before any work begins. No surprise charges, ever.
