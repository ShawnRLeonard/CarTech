Family-Owned & Built on Integrity

CarTech Automotive has been serving Smyrna and the surrounding Middle Tennessee community since 2020. We're a family-run shop — not a franchise, not a chain — and every car that comes through our doors gets treated like it belongs to one of our own.

In 2024 we expanded with a dedicated state-of-the-art paint & body booth, bringing precision color matching and collision repair fully in-house. Whether it's a routine oil change or a full collision repair, we do the job right the first time. No guesswork, no shortcuts.
