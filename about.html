About CarTech Automotive

Since CarTech Automotive was established in 2020, we've built our reputation on providing honest, reliable, and high-quality auto repair. We are committed to excellence, never compromising on quality. We ensure that every replacement part meets OEM standards and take the time to do the job right, without ever cutting corners. Your vehicle deserves nothing less than the best.

In 2024, we maximized our space utilization by the addition of a dedicated Paint & Body section. This addition includes a state-of-the-art paint booth, advanced color code scanning technology, and software integration that ensures a flawless color match every time. With over 65 high-quality automotive color options available in-house, we're equipped to blend seamlessly with any color you need.

Our team is equipped with advanced diagnostic tools and top-quality equipment, enabling us to work on all makes and models of vehicles. We take pride in our commitment to customer satisfaction and maintaining transparent pricing.

At CarTech, we're not just fixing cars - we're building lasting relationships with our customers. We put our customers first, every time. We are a family-run and operated shop built on integrity. We know that your vehicle is a crucial part of your daily life, and we're dedicated to keeping it running smoothly and safely and looking its best. Thank you for your business!
