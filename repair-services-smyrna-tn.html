Auto Repair & Diagnostics in Smyrna, TN

A warning light on your dash. A sound that wasn't there last week. Something that just feels off. Whatever's happening with your vehicle, CarTech Automotive has the diagnostic equipment and the trained technicians to figure it out accurately — without guessing.

We service all makes and models, domestic and import. Our process starts with a thorough diagnostic scan and visual inspection. We identify the root cause — not just the symptom — and we explain everything we find in plain language before we touch a single bolt. No surprise charges, no unnecessary repairs, no pressure.

From check engine lights and TPMS warnings to AC system failures and electrical shorts, we handle the full range of repair services that keep your vehicle safe and running the way it should.

Why Choose CarTech for Repair & Diagnostics

  • Advanced diagnostic equipment — we use professional-grade scan tools and live data analysis to find the actual cause, not just clear the code.
  • Check engine & all dash warning lights — we diagnose every warning light including check engine, TPMS, ABS, traction control, and more.
  • AC & heat repair — refrigerant leaks, compressor failures, heater core issues, blend door problems — we handle it all.
  • Electrical diagnosis & repair — shorts, bad grounds, parasitic drains, wiring issues on all platforms including imports.
  • Transparent process — we explain exactly what we found and what it costs before any repair begins. You approve everything.
  • All makes and models — domestic and import, cars, trucks, and SUVs. One shop for everything.
