Hyundai & Kia Services We Cover

🔍

Diagnostics & Check Engine

Check engine light, TPMS sensors, ABS faults, transmission warnings, and all Hyundai and Kia-specific diagnostic codes. We use advanced scan tools to identify the issue accurately — no guesswork.

🕔

Routine Maintenance

Oil changes to Hyundai/Kia spec, brake pads and rotors, tire rotation, spark plugs, cabin and engine air filters, and coolant service. We follow the factory maintenance schedule to protect your powertrain warranty.

⚙️

Engine Service

Oil consumption inspection, timing chain, spark plugs, valve cover gaskets, serpentine belt, water pump, and all Hyundai/Kia Theta II, Nu, and Smartstream engine repairs. Honest diagnosis, no unnecessary work.

🚘

Transmission & DCT Service

Hyundai/Kia dual-clutch (DCT) fluid service, 6-speed and 8-speed automatic transmission service, IVT/CVT service, and minor repairs — using the correct OEM-spec fluids every time.

🌟

Paint & Collision Repair

Parking lot dings, bumper damage, side swipes, or full collision repair — our dedicated body shop uses computerized color matching for all Hyundai and Kia colors. Free written estimates.

🔊

Noise & Drivability

DCT shudder on acceleration, oil consumption knock, transmission hesitation, vibration — Hyundai and Kia-specific issues that need accurate diagnosis. We find the source and fix it right.
