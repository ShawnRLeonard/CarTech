Engine Repair & Service in Smyrna, TN

Your engine is the most complex and most critical system in your vehicle. When something isn't right — whether it's a warning light, a loss of power, an unusual noise, or something you just can't explain — CarTech Automotive has the diagnostic tools and the technical experience to find the problem and fix it correctly.

We service all makes and models, domestic and import, from minor tune-ups and spark plug replacements to major internal repairs like head gaskets, timing belt systems, and complete engine rebuilds. Every repair starts with a thorough diagnosis — we identify the root cause before recommending a solution, and we explain everything clearly before we begin.

Don't wait on engine symptoms. What starts as a small issue can quickly become a much more expensive repair. Bring it in early and let us tell you exactly what's going on.

Why Choose CarTech for Engine Service

  • Full range of engine repairs — tune-ups, spark plugs, ignition coils, fuel injectors, timing belts/chains, head gaskets, and full rebuilds.
  • Timing belt & chain service — one of the most critical preventive repairs. We replace the belt, tensioner, idler pulleys, and water pump as a complete job.
  • Advanced diagnostics first — we identify the actual root cause before recommending any repair. No guessing, no unnecessary parts swaps.
  • OEM-quality parts — we use parts that meet or exceed manufacturer specifications on every engine repair.
  • Experienced with domestic and import engines — Nissan, Toyota, Honda, Ford, Chevrolet, Subaru, and more. One shop for everything.
  • Clear estimates, no surprises — you get a full breakdown of what the repair involves and what it costs before we touch your engine.
