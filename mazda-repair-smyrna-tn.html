Mazda Services We Cover

🔍

Diagnostics & Check Engine

Check engine light, SkyActiv engine fault codes, i-ACTIV AWD system warnings, TPMS sensors, and all Mazda-specific diagnostic codes. We identify the issue accurately before recommending any repair.

🕔

Routine Maintenance

Oil changes to Mazda spec (0W-20 full synthetic for SkyActiv engines), brake pads and rotors, tire rotation and balance, cabin and engine air filters, and coolant service following Mazda's maintenance schedule.

⚙️

SkyActiv Engine Service

Timing chain service, spark plug replacement, valve cover gaskets, water pump, and serpentine belt on all SkyActiv-G engines. Carbon deposit cleaning is especially important on direct-injection SkyActiv engines over 60k miles.

🚘

Transmission & AWD Service

Automatic transmission fluid service, manual gearbox (MX-5 Miata 6-speed), and i-ACTIV AWD rear differential service. Proper fluid intervals prevent costly transmission and AWD wear on high-mileage CX-5s and Mazda3 AWD models.

🌟

Paint & Collision Repair

Bumper dings, fender damage, or full collision repair — our dedicated body shop uses computerized color matching for all Mazda colors, including the deep-flake Soul Red Crystal and Machine Gray metallic finishes. Free written estimates.

🔊

Noise & Drivability

Vibration at highway speed, rough idle, clunks over bumps, or hesitation under acceleration — we diagnose the root cause precisely. G-Vectoring Control (GVC) issues, CV axle noise, and suspension concerns all diagnosed correctly the first time.
