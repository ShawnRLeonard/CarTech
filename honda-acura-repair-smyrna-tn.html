Honda & Acura Services We Cover

🔍

Diagnostics & Check Engine

VTC actuator codes, VTEC system faults, transmission warning lights, TPMS, ABS, and all Honda-specific diagnostic codes. We pinpoint the issue precisely — no parts-throwing.

🕔

Routine Maintenance

Oil changes (conventional, synthetic, or full synthetic to Honda spec), brake pads and rotors, tire rotation, cabin and engine air filters, coolant flush. We follow Honda Maintenance Minder intervals.

⚙️

Engine & Timing Service

Timing belt replacement (older V6 models), timing chain, VTC actuator, head gaskets, water pump, spark plugs, and all VTEC and i-VTEC engine repairs for all Honda and Acura powertrains.

🚘

CVT & Transmission

Honda CVT fluid service (must use Honda HCF-2 spec fluid), automatic and manual transmission service, and repairs. We handle everything from fluid changes to full transmission service.

🌟

Paint & Collision Repair

Parking lot dings, fender damage, side swipes, or full collision — our dedicated body shop uses computerized color matching to blend your Honda's finish perfectly. Free estimates.

🔊

Noise & Drivability

VTC actuator rattle on cold start, transmission shudder on CVT models, vibration, rough idle — we find the source and fix it right. No unnecessary repairs, no guesswork.
